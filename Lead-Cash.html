What is Lead-to-Cash?

L2C is the complete process from customer interest to revenue collection. Companies with optimized L2C achieve 36% higher revenue growth and 28% more profitability.

Why It Matters

  • Revenue Acceleration: Close deals 27% faster
  • Reduced Leakage: Cut errors by 40%
  • Better Win Rates: Increase wins 15-25%
  • Improved Cash Flow: Faster collections
  • Enhanced CX: 3.5x higher retention

8 Success Factors

  • Executive Sponsorship: CRO/CEO must champion
  • Unified Metrics: Shared outcomes
  • Single Source of Truth: CRM is record
