Team Expansion Discovery Workshop

Strategic hiring and organizational design to scale
from 5 to 50+ employees

3 Hour Collaborative Session

Session Overview

1

Organizational Design & Structure

Design your organizational chart, reporting lines, and role definitions. Establish clear accountability and prevent gaps or overlaps as you scale rapidly.

2

Hiring Prioritization & Strategy

Define hiring sequence, role requirements, and candidate profiles. Align talent acquisition with revenue goals and budget constraints over 12-24 months.

3

Performance & Culture Framework

Establish core values, performance metrics, and people management systems. Build foundation for culture that scales beyond the founding team.

4

Growth Blocker Analysis

Identify the 1-2 critical obstacles preventing effective hiring and team scaling in the coming year.

Session Outcomes

You'll leave with a clear hiring roadmap and organizational structure to support 3-5x headcount growth.

BearOps will deliver a customized proposal outlining org design, hiring processes, and performance management systems.
