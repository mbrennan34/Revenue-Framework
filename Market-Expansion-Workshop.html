Market Expansion Discovery Workshop

Strategic framework for entering new geographic markets
or customer segments

3 Hour Collaborative Session

Session Overview

1

Market Opportunity Assessment

Evaluate target market size, competition, customer demand, and entry barriers. Validate assumptions about market readiness and product-market fit.

2

Go-to-Market Strategy

Define market entry approach: direct sales, partnerships, digital-first, or hybrid. Establish pricing, positioning, and channel strategy for new market.

3

Operational & Resource Planning

Identify requirements for legal entities, compliance, hiring, infrastructure, and budget. Create realistic timeline and resource allocation for market entry.

4

Growth Blocker Analysis

Identify the 1-2 critical obstacles preventing successful market expansion over the next 12-18 months.

Session Outcomes

You'll leave with a validated market entry strategy and realistic resource requirements.

BearOps will deliver a customized proposal outlining expansion roadmap, GTM playbooks, and operational requirements.
