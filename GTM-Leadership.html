GTM Leadership Discovery Workshop

Set up new revenue leader for success with clarity,
structure, and quick wins

3 Hour Collaborative Session

Session Overview

1

Revenue Strategy & ICP Definition

Clarify revenue targets, market positioning, and ideal customer profile. Align leadership on GTM strategy and revenue model for the next 12-24 months.

2

GTM Process & Infrastructure Audit

Map current sales and marketing processes, tech stack, and handoff points. Identify gaps between current state and what's needed to achieve revenue goals.

3

90-Day Onboarding & Success Plan

Define clear expectations, quick wins, and success metrics for new revenue leader's first 90 days. Establish authority, resources, and support structure.

4

Growth Blocker Analysis

Identify the 1-2 critical obstacles preventing revenue acceleration and new leader success over the next 6-12 months.

Session Outcomes

You'll leave with a clear 90-day plan and defined success metrics for your new revenue leader.

BearOps will deliver a customized proposal outlining GTM processes, playbooks, and infrastructure to support revenue growth.
